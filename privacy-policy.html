Privacy Policy - KoordKonvert

Last Updated: March 20, 2025

The developer of the KoordKonvert app is committed to protecting user data. This policy outlines how we handle data

Collected Data

Location data (optional, for map and conversion purposes)

Purchase history (Google Play Billing, for ad-free version)

Saved coordinates (stored in a local SQLite database)

Device identifiers (for Google Ads advertising)

Use of Data

To ensure the app’s functionality (e.g., maps, coordinate saving).

To display advertisements (Google Ads).

Data Sharing

Google LLC (Maps API, Ads, Billing) for functionality and advertising purposes.

Data Protection

Data is transmitted encrypted via HTTPS.

Data Deletion

There is currently no separate deletion feature, but local data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

Contact: koordkonvert@gmail.com

Gyűjtött adatok

Helyadatok (opcionális, térkép és konvertálás céljából)
